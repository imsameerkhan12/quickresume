Resume Headline for Freshers: 20 Examples

A resume headline is a one-line label under your name — and on job portals like Naukri it's a searchable field recruiters filter by. A good one packs your degree, top skill and target role into ~10 words.

The formula

[Degree/status] + [top skills] + [target role]. No full sentences needed.

IT / engineering

B.Tech CSE Fresher | Java, SQL, DSA | Software Developer
Frontend Developer Fresher | React, JavaScript, 3 Live Projects
B.Tech ECE Graduate | Embedded C, IoT Projects | Trainee Engineer
MCA Fresher | Python, Django | Backend Developer Roles
Diploma Mechanical | AutoCAD, SolidWorks | Design Trainee

Commerce / finance

B.Com Graduate | Tally, GST, Advanced Excel | Accounts Executive
CA Inter | Audit & Taxation Knowledge | Article Assistant
MBA Finance Fresher | Financial Modelling Basics | Analyst Roles

BPO / support / operations

Graduate Fresher | Fluent English & Hindi | Customer Support (Voice)
12th Pass | 45 WPM Typing, CRM Basics | Data Entry / Back Office
Hotel Management Graduate | Front Office & Guest Relations

Marketing / content / design

MBA Marketing Fresher | Digital Marketing Certified | Executive Roles
B.A. English | SEO Content Writing, 50+ Published Articles
B.Des Graduate | Figma, Canva, Brand Design | UI/UX Intern

Science / teaching / other

B.Sc. Chemistry | Lab Techniques, Documentation | QC Trainee
B.Ed. + M.A. | CBSE Curriculum | TGT English
B.Pharm Fresher | GMP Knowledge | Production / QA Roles
BBA Graduate | MS Office, Communication | Management Trainee
Data Analyst Aspirant | Excel, SQL, Power BI Certified
Law Graduate (LL.B.) | Legal Research & Drafting | Junior Associate
✅ On Naukri, include exact keywords recruiters search ("Java", "Tally", "voice process") — the headline is indexed by their search engine.
