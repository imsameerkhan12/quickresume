How to Make a Resume on Your Phone (Free, No App)

No laptop? No problem. You can make a clean, professional PDF resume entirely on your phone — no app download, no sign-up, no payment. Here's exactly how.

Open the free resume builder →

Step-by-step (works on Android and iPhone)

  1. Open the builder in Chrome or Safari — the button above. It runs in the browser; nothing installs.
  2. Tap "Load sample" to see the correct format, then replace the sample text with your details.
  3. Fill in the sections: name, a 1–2 line summary, education, skills. Add experience or projects if you have them.
  4. Pick a template — Classic and Minimal are safest for most jobs.
  5. Tap "Download PDF". On Android, choose Save as PDF in the print menu. On iPhone, tap Share → Save to Files.

Where does the PDF go?

  • Android: usually in the Downloads folder — attach it in WhatsApp, email or job portals from there.
  • iPhone: in the Files app under the location you chose.

Phone resume tips

✅ Your data stays on your phone — this builder uploads nothing, so it's safe on shared/borrowed devices too (just use the Clear button when done).
  • Type your summary in a notes app first if you find the small box fiddly.
  • Autosave means you can fill half now and finish later on the same phone.
  • Sending on WhatsApp? Send as a document, not a photo, so it stays sharp.
