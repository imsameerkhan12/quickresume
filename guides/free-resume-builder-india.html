What makes a good Indian resume in 2026

  • One page for freshers, up to two for experienced candidates.
  • Simple, single-column layout so applicant tracking systems (ATS) can read it.
  • A short summary at the top naming the role you want.
  • Achievements, not duties — "increased X by Y%" beats "responsible for X".
  • Contact details: phone, professional email, city, LinkedIn.
